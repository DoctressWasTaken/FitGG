FitGG

Working out while gaming.

What's it all about?

FitGG is an app hosted through overwolf that provides you with a full workout throughout your gaming day.
By tracking the games flow we provide you with exercises just when the time is right. Whenever...

..your turn ends in Hearthstone..
..you heroically sacrifice yourself for your team in CS:GO..
..you get unexpectedly 5 man dove in League of Legends..
..your Army battles for you in TFT..
or just simply inbetween matches..
..we will give you a small exercise fitting the time available before you need to respond to the game again.

And in the time it takes your team to make you lose a rank you will have completed a full set of exercises.
